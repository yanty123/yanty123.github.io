About me
------

I am currently a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the State Key Laboratory of Internet of Things for Smart City, University of Macau. I am fortunate to be supervised by [Prof. Jianbing Shen](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=_Q3NTToAAAAJ&view_op=list_works&sortby=pubdate) and [Prof. Cheng-zhong Xu](https://scholar.google.com.hk/citations?user=XsBBTUgAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN). My current research interests primarily focus on World Models (Simulation and Post-training), Physical AI and Autonomous Driving.

Previously, I received my Master's degree from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) and the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), under the supervision of [Prof. Jinqiao Wang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=zh-CN&user=7_BkyxEAAAAJ&view_op=list_works&sortby=pubdate) and [Prof. Ming Tang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=woezF_MAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN). I obtained my Bachelor from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in 2020.

I am always open for research discussions and collaborations. 

Recently, I have also been looking for research intern opportunities related to the physical-aware world model and diffusion policy closed-loop training.
